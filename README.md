# PSX Sector Power Rankings

## Business Question
Which sectors actually drove the PSX's FY26 rally, and how consistent 
were the top contributors month over month?

Dashboard Preview<img width="1246" height="700" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/40acb33e-674f-47da-a413-352c410ab48b" />


## Data Source
Compiled from Mettis Global and INP/WealthPK monthly PSX sector 
reports, Apr–Jun 2026, plus FY26 full-year aggregates.

## Key Finding
Commercial Banks and Cement appeared among the top 3 point-contributing 
sectors in all three months, making them the most consistent drivers 
of the rally — while Fertilizer's contribution spiked specifically in 
May and June.
